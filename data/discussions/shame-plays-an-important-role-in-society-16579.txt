Discussion Title: Shame plays an important role in society.

1. Shame plays an important role in society.
1.1. Pro: Keeps people conforming.
1.1.1. Pro: Shame is one of the [biggest fears](https://www.marieclaire.co.uk/life/public-shaming-497591)
1.1.1.1. Pro: This is especially true [for women.](https://whenwomenspeak.net/blog/womens-shame-1/)
1.2. Con: A form of control that suppresses individuality
1.2.1. Pro: Shame is used to make people behave a certain way. This behavior is usually dictated by the powerful in society and is not necessarily always right.
1.2.2. Pro: Society is more interesting when it is diverse, which only happens when people are able to be themselves, rather than be controlled by others.
1.2.3. Con: Suppressing some aspects of individuality is not necessarily a bad thing, as there are some types of behavior we generally don't want to encourage in society.
1.3. Pro: Ensures people does the right thing
1.3.1. Pro: There are particular norms in society, such as civility and kindness, that it is valuable to uphold. One of the best ways of doing this is by shaming those who do not adhere to these behaviors.
1.4. Con: Creates feelings of guilt
1.4.1. Pro: People are unlikely to feel like they can take chances in society when they fear social sanctions in the form of being made to feel ashamed and guilty.
1.5. Pro: Ensures religions are maintained
1.6. Con: Can create mental health problems
1.7. Pro: Provides a useful tool to teach people right from wrong
1.7.1. Pro: Shame can be an important feeling to experience in developing an understanding of how we should behave and treat others.
1.8. Con: Culturally constructed and meaningless
