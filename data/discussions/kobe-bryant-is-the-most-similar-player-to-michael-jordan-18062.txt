Discussion Title: Kobe Bryant is the most similar player to Michael Jordan

1. Kobe Bryant is the most similar player to Michael Jordan.
1.1. Con: LeBron has the ability to surpass Kobe in the all-time scoring list.
1.2. Con: LeBron has broken some Kobe's records.
1.2.1. Con: Lebron breaking a record doesn't equate to Michael and Kobe being dissimilar.
1.2.1.1. Pro: Its quite possible Lebron could be superior than both of them in the specific area of playing which he broke the record.
1.3. Pro: Former players claim that Kobe Bryant is 'closest thing' to Michael Jordan.
1.3.1. Pro: [Steve Kerr](https://www.cbssports.com/nba/news/steve-kerr-explains-why-kobe-bryant-not-lebron-is-closest-thing-to-michael-jordan/) \(former Jordan teammate\) states that Kobe's footwork was very similar to Michael Jordan's - "Kobe is the closest thing to Michael \(Jordan\)," Kerr said. "Everybody's been compared to Michael. LeBron's been compared to Michael -- I don't think LeBron is Michael at all. I think he's a very different player, a very different mentality and mindset. Kobe has the same mentality and mindset that MJ had"
1.3.2. Pro: [Dwyane Wade](http://www.basketballinsiders.com/dwyane-wade-kobe-bryant-is-our-michael-jordan/) \(former LeBron teammate\) - “For this generation, Kobe \(Bryant\) is our Michael Jordan \[..\]”
1.3.3. Con: Many of these players have not played against Lebron to know the comparison first hand.
1.4. Con: With [1143 games](https://www.basketball-reference.com/players/j/jamesle01.html), LeBron is closer to Jordan's [1072 games](https://www.basketball-reference.com/players/j/jordami01.html) than Kobe with [1346 games](https://www.basketball-reference.com/players/b/bryanko01.html).
1.4.1. Con: The number of games played doesn't convey anything about the similarity of the two as players.
1.5. Pro: Their playing style and signature moves are very similar.
1.5.1. Pro: Kobe has the advantage of playing with Jordan, and can therefore mimic many of his moves.
1.5.1.1. Con: With 21st Century access to media and information, you don't need to play with someone to mimic his moves.
1.5.2. Pro: A visual comparision proves their similarities [Watch video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v27Hk5OIe-k)
1.6. Pro: Kobe has the same mentality \(Mamba Mentality\) that MJ had: to constantly try to be the best version, to try to better today than yesterday.
1.7. Pro: They played in same position \(shooting-guard\).
1.7.1. Con: Playing the same position is a similarity many players in the field have in common. This alone does not make Kobe most similar to Michael Jordan.
1.8. Pro: Both were [clutch](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clutch_\(sports\)), supreme players on last seconds of the game.
1.9. Pro: Kobe, like Jordan, was a top athlete finishing at the rim and both proved it in the Slam Dunk Contest.
1.9.1. Con: Many players have done well, winning the Slam Dunk Contest. By this standard, any player that has done well can be considered similar to Michael Jordan.
1.9.2. Con: Lebron James never participated in the Slam Dunk Contest, so it is impossible to know whether he would have won.
1.9.2.1. Pro: Lebron [intended](https://bleacherreport.com/articles/2760026-lebron-james-says-he-intended-to-enter-slam-dunk-contest-in-previous-years) to participate, but was nursing injuries at the time.
1.9.3. Pro: [Both Kobe and Michael were top finishers impressing with their dunks.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VRTJV_wfAes)
1.9.4. Pro: Both [Michael Jordan](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uYE-lT5WEPM) and [Kobe Bryant](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bk3Xd-A61j0) were top finishers with the circus shots.
1.10. Pro: Kobe was a top defensive guard, as was Jordan.
1.10.1. Pro: Kobe Bryant has made a total of 12 All-Defensive teams, more than any other guard
