Discussion Title: The Baphomet statue should be installed at the Arkansas Court house

1. The Baphomet statue should be installed at the Arkansas Court house
1.1. Pro: Requiring legislative sponsorship gives the government the power to decide which religions are acceptable in society.
1.1.1. Con: Legislative sponsorship is a proxy for the representation of this particular belief in the community.
1.2. Pro: Satanism reflects many of society's values.
1.2.1. Pro: [Jex Blackmore](http://time.com/3972713/detroit-satanic-statue-baphomet/), director of the Detroit Satanic Temple chapter describes Baphomet as containing 'binary elements symbolizing a reconciliation of opposites, emblematic of the willingness to embrace, and even celebrate differences.'
1.2.2. Pro: Satanism is a philosophy of individual expression and freedom from religious repression.
1.3. Con: There should be no statues of a religious nature on public grounds.
1.3.1. Pro: The [First Amendment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Separation_of_church_and_state_in_the_United_States#The_First_Amendment) of the U.S. Constitution states, 'Congress shall make no law respecting an establishment of religion, or prohibiting the free exercise thereof...'
1.3.1.1. Pro: This means that the secularism of the state is openly recognized by the Constitution.
1.3.1.1.1. Pro: Secularism is an important feature of democracy.
1.4. Con: The Satanic statue is being proposed for political, not religious reasons. Unless the belief is sincere, the statue should not be installed.
1.4.1. Con: The only one whom can gauge the sincerity of a belief is the holder. As such, sincerity cannot be a deciding factor.
1.5. Con: There has been no legislative sponsorship for the statue, which is required by law.
1.5.1. Con: -> See 1.1.
1.6. Pro: To have one religious statue and not others is a violation of the First Amendment, which states that Congress shall make no law respecting an establishment of religion.
1.7. Pro: If one religious statue is allowed to stand, all should be allowed to stand.
1.7.1. Con: Satanism is a detrimental belief.
