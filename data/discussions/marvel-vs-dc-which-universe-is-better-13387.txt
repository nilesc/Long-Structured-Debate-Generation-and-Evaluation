Discussion Title: Marvel vs DC: Which Universe is Better?

1. Marvel Universe is Better than DC Universe
1.1. Con: DC has more legacy characters.
1.1.1. Con: The fact that a character has been around longer does not prove that they are of a higher quality.
1.1.2. Pro: [Superman](https://www.theguardian.com/culture/2013/jan/05/why-is-superman-so-popular) has been a well-known character since 1938, standing the test of time.
1.1.3. Pro: [Batman and Superman](https://www.ranker.com/list/superheroes-ranked-by-most-comic-book-appearances/ranker-comics) are the two most popular superheroes.
1.1.3.1. Con: DC may have the top-two most-used movie characters, but their movies lineup has been [overly top-heavy and inconsistent.](https://observer.com/2018/06/most-popular-superhero-marvel-vs-dc-batman-superman-wolverine-spider-man/)
1.2. Con: DC has more diversity among its characters.
1.2.1. Con: Diversity doesn't necessarily make something better. Shakespeare for example has some of the most renowned works in English. But they're not very diverse.
1.2.1.1. Con: The Marvel Universe was modeled and copied from the DC universe. Marvel wouldn’t exist without DC.
1.2.2. Con: Marvel has created a vast quantity of characters wich a various range of people can relate to. It even has become difficult to find a minority not depicted by a Marvel hero \(or vilains\)
1.2.3. Pro: Marvel’s characters are mostly from one city, New York City, while DC's characters are more spread out.
1.3. Con: DC superheroes are more well-known.
1.3.1. Con: With the advent of the Marvel film franchises Marvel's characters are arguably more well known.
1.3.2. Con: Even if this claim is true, ubiquity does not mean quality.
1.3.2.1. Pro: Being most famous does not translate into being better.
1.3.3. Pro: -> See 1.1.3.
1.3.4. Con: Marvel has [8 characters](https://www.ranker.com/list/superheroes-ranked-by-most-comic-book-appearances/ranker-comics) in the top 10 of most popular superheroes.
1.3.5. Pro: [Wonder Woman](https://www.thetoptens.com/best-female-superheroes/) is the number one ranked best female superhero
1.4. Pro: Marvel stories contain better characters.
1.4.1. Pro: Marvel characters contain more [human flaws](https://www.polygon.com/2017/7/27/16050214/marvel-dc-spider-man).
1.4.1.1. Con: DC has started to add more human flaws in their film adaptations, for example with Batman or Wonder Woman.
1.4.1.2. Pro: Many of Marvel's characters are shown dealing with normal human challenges, like social awkwardness and break-ups.
1.4.1.3. Pro: The personality and experiences the characters overcome makes them relatable.
1.4.2. Pro: Marvel characters are more relatable to the average person.
1.4.3. Pro: DC characters are either too powerful or too weak to relate to.
1.4.3.1. Pro: In the Justice League, compared to Superman, every other hero is powerless.
1.4.3.1.1. Pro: [Batman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Batman#Abilities), for example, has no superpowers.
1.4.3.1.2. Con: DC characters are able to overcome a lot without powers, making them more impressive in this area.
1.4.3.1.3. Pro: [Wonder Woman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wonder_Woman#Abilities) relies on her lasso and wrist shields, but wields no inherent powers herself.
1.4.3.1.4. Con: [Aquaman](https://www.dccomics.com/characters/aquaman) has the ability to communicate and control sea life.
1.4.3.2. Con: Women [related](https://www.wired.com/2017/06/wonder-woman-hero-moment/) to the power and strength of Wonder Woman.
1.4.3.3. Con: -> See 1.4.1.3.
1.5. Pro: The modern Marvel Cinematic Universe is a much higher quality compared to DC's.
1.5.1. Pro: Marvel has spent the time and energy to write a relatively consistent cinematic universe with no compromises on character development.  DC has done the opposite and has impatiently rushed to create a cinematic universe to catch up with Marvel's, but failed to meet them on any metric.
1.6. Pro: Marvel stories contain more exciting events.
1.6.1. Pro: DC stories are not as interesting or believable
1.6.1.1. Con: Marvel stories are not believable as well.
1.6.1.2. Con: Both Marvel and DC stories are about fictional superheroes and characters achieving unrealistic feats. They're not created to be believable, but entertaining.
1.6.1.3. Con: What is interesting or believable is subjective.
1.6.2. Con: The excitement of an event is subjective, as one person may consider DC more exciting.
1.7. Con: DC isn't afraid to show the darker side of humanity.
1.7.1. Con: This argument is only relevant for cinematic universes. Regarding shows and comics, Marvel isn't afraid exploring or depicting the darkests traits of human being \(Killjoy in Jessica Jones, Vilains in Luke Cage, Villains in Iron fist\). Not to mention "Logan" movie, or the latests arcs...
1.7.2. Pro: [Sexual assault](http://dc.wikia.com/wiki/Sexual_assault) is not uncommon in the DC universe.
1.8. Pro: Marvel built much of their universe based on real places and events which makes it easier to relate to.
1.8.1. Pro: Black Panther was based on a combination of [real African nations](https://www.inverse.com/article/40961-black-panther-wakanda-real-life-inspirations-africa), such as Nigeria, the Congo, and Kenya.
1.8.2. Pro: In Iron Man 2, Tony Stark sits atop [Randy's Donuts](http://marvelcinematicuniverse.wikia.com/wiki/Randy%27s_Donuts), a popular attraction in Inglewood, California.
1.8.3. Con: The Marvel Universe is built on fictional events and places, such as [Wakanda](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wakanda).
1.8.4. Con: [Gotham City](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gotham_City) is based on [Chicago](http://www.today.com/id/25774413/ns/today-today_entertainment/t/dark-knights-kind-town-gotham-city/).
1.9. Pro: Marvel stories intertwine better between different movies.
1.9.1. Con: DC stories do not naturally overlap.
1.9.1.1. Pro: DC characters follow a less interconnected storyline, making each of their paths unique.
1.9.2. Pro: Thor, The Avengers, Captain America, Iron Man, Dr. Strange and Black Panther have storylines that all intersect.
1.9.2.1. Pro: [Captain America Civil War](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Captain_America:_Civil_War), revealed the killing of Tony Starks' \(Iron Man\) parents, were at the hands of the Winter Soldier.
1.10. Pro: Stan Lee's Marvel vision contains more clarity and purpose than DC.
1.10.1. Pro: DC is simply interested in churning entertainment to the masses.
