Discussion Title: Should I #DeleteFacebook Account?

1. Should I #DeleteFacebook account?
1.1. Pro: Facebook is no longer a place where people 'catch up' and socialise with one another.
1.1.1. Pro: Facebook instead indulges a false reality via pre-approved filtered content mostly from bigger companies trying to sell to Facebook users.
1.2. Pro: Facebook consumes a lot of time.
1.2.1. Con: A person can exercise self-discipline so they are not on Facebook all the time.
1.3. Con: Deleting Facebook is an individual action that will have no effect on the collective problem of privacy
1.4. Pro: Facebook is full of spam content from brands.
1.4.1. Con: Most [social media](http://techcrunchies.com/growth-of-social-media-spam-statistics/), like Twitter and Youtube have spam content.
1.5. Pro: Personal data is not secure on Facebook.
1.6. Pro: There are better social media options.
1.6.1. Con: People use social media based on their personal preference and needs. What is better is a matter of subjectivity.
1.6.2. Con: Facebook is the most well-known and trusted social media platform.
1.6.3. Pro: Watsapp
1.7. Pro: Nobody actually is there to socialize.
1.7.1. Pro: Facebook's founder [recently shared](https://contently.com/strategist/2016/04/12/facebook-isnt-social-network-anymore/) that its not a place to socialize anymore, its becoming a place to share content and things you want.
1.8. Con: Deleting Facebook will make a person lose contact with their friends and online associates.
1.8.1. Con: Those who do not wish to use Facebook to keep up with friends can utilize other non-social media methods such as Skype, Google Hangout, or Zoom.
1.8.1.1. Con: Friends are often not willing to move to new platforms just to keep up with one person.
1.8.2. Con: There are other social media platforms one can use to stay in touch with friends.
1.8.2.1. Pro: Snapchat and Instagram are common Facebook alternatives for staying connected to friends and family.
1.8.3. Pro: Facebook opens its users up to a world of new people they otherwise would not know, locally and globally.
1.9. Con: Facebook is a place to see the latest news. This will be lost if deleted.
1.10. Con: There is no other option to socialize on the web.
1.10.1. Con: -> See 1.8.2.
