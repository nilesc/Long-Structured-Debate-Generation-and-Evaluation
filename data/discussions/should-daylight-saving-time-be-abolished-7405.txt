Discussion Title: Should Daylight Saving Time Be Abolished?

1. Daylight saving time should be kept
1.1. Con: DST disturbs rest-activity cycle.
1.1.1. Pro: Daylight savings time can contribute to [sleep deprivation](https://www.livescience.com/11060-daylight-saving-americans-bodies-reset.html).
1.1.1.1. Pro: The 1 hour difference has been shown to [affect](http://www.smrv-journal.com/article/S1087-0792\(12\)00114-1/abstract) the sleep cycle for up to a week.
1.2. Con: Changing times makes it more difficult to connect with people in other parts of the world when their time stays the same.
1.2.1. Con: This is untrue: the advancements in communication outweigh any problems figuring out what time it is in another country.
1.3. Pro: Daylight Saving Time saves energy.
1.3.1. Con: In the days of oil lamps and 1 shift work, maybe.
It's a 24/7 world, with people working 2nd and 3rd shifts. Stores are open 24/7. Some people sleep during the day. Daylight savings is obsolete.
1.3.2. Pro: Less Artificial light is used thanks to DST
1.3.2.1. Con: Not in today's 24/7 world.
1.4. Con: The older you get the more time it takes your body to adjust to the time change
1.5. Pro: Daylight Saving Time has a positive effect on the economy.
1.6. Pro: DST reduces crime.
1.6.1. Con: Assault rates are [higher](https://www.livescience.com/60806-daylight-saving-time-linked-to-assault.html) on the Monday following Day Light Savings time.
1.6.2. Con: [Suicide rates](http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1479-8425.2007.00331.x/abstract) are higher weeks following DST due to shifts in the diurnal rhythms.
1.7. Con: Changing the time takes some effort and has to be remembered.
1.7.1. Pro: If people from the Northern and Southern hemisphere communicate, they may have to change time-differences four times a year, since shifts go in opposite directions and may take effect at different weekends.
1.8. Con: DST impacts health negatively.
1.8.1. Pro: Researchers found a 25% increase in[heart attacks](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/study-ties-daylight-saving-time-change-to-rise-in-heart-attacks/) the Monday following DST.
1.9. Pro: DST decreases the number of traffic accidents.
1.9.1. Con: There is actually a 17% increase in the number of care accidents on the day on DST [telegram.com](http://www.telegram.com/article/20150306/NEWS/303069496)
