Discussion Title: Ketchup is a child’s condiment.

1. Ketchup is a child’s condiment.
1.1. Pro: Ketchup may [stain your teeth](https://www.express.co.uk/life-style/health/718700/Food-drink-avoid-white-teeth-dentist-Ketchup-wine-fruit-tea).
1.2. Con: Many foods are eaten by both children, and adults.
1.3. Pro: It tastes gross.
1.3.1. Con: Ketchup contains flavor compounds for [sweet, sour, salty, bitter, and umami](https://www.newyorker.com/magazine/2004/09/06/the-ketchup-conundrum), the five known fundamental human tastes.
1.3.2. Con: Taste is a subjective matter.
1.3.3. Con: Genetic [differences](https://www.popsci.com/science/article/2012-03/fyi-why-does-some-food-taste-bad-some-people-and-good-others) between people mean that foods 'taste' different, in that they elicit different sensory responses.
1.4. Pro: It smells gross.
1.4.1. Con: The sense of smell differs for different people, so what is gross for one person might smell delicious for another.
1.5. Pro: [Sensitivity](https://recipes.howstuffworks.com/food-facts/kids-love-ketchup.htm) to bitter flavors may explain why children prefer sweet flavorings like ketchup during their formative years.
1.5.1. Con: Many children get [addicted to sugar](https://www.huffingtonpost.com/david-sack-md/kids-sugar_b_3862244.html) early, and crave any food containing it.
1.6. Con: According to international [consumption](https://www.statista.com/outlook/40070100/109/tomato-ketchup/united-states#market-onlineRevenueShare) data, adults do like and eat ketchup in considerable quantities.
1.7. Con: There is [evidence](https://www.telegraph.co.uk/men/active/mens-health/10874557/Tomato-ketchup-five-surprising-health-benefits.html) that ketchup may be good for your health.
1.7.1. Con: Ketchup is very high in processed [sugars](https://www.huffingtonpost.com/2015/05/18/ketchup-sugar-per-serving_n_7293052.html?guccounter=1), which makes it very unhealthy.
