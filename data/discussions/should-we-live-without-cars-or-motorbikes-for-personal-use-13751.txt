Discussion Title: Should We Live Without Cars or Motorbikes for Personal Use?

1. We Should Live Without Cars or Motorbikes for Personal Use.
1.1. Pro: It would reduce pollution, making the air clearer.
1.1.1. Con: Electric cars could solve the noise and pollution problems.
1.2. Con: Our public transport system wouldn't be adapted enough to this to make it work.
1.3. Pro: It would reduce noise pollution.
1.3.1. Con: -> See 1.1.1.
1.4. Pro: It would encourage more locally-based working.
1.4.1. Con: Some jobs cannot be locally based; for instance, a commercial agent who needs to meet customers around the country.
1.5. Pro: It would mean less time wasted sitting in traffic.
1.6. Con: It would make it difficult for individuals to move over longer distances.
1.6.1. Con: We can travel by train, which would become cheaper because of the increased usage.
1.6.2. Pro: Travel to and from rural areas, too remote for train stops or frequent buses, would become very difficult.
1.7. Con: There would be an economic impact from reduced sales and associated loss of jobs.
1.8. Con: It would be almost impossible to implement.
1.8.1. Con: Enforcing bans or prison terms for those using vehicles for personal use might be reasonably implementable.
