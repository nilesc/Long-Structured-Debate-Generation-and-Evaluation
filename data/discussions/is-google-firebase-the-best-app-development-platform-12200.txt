Discussion Title: Is Google Firebase the best app development platform?

1. Firebase is the best application development platform.
1.1. Pro: Firebase has good security.
1.1.1. Pro: If the website itself gets breached, they wont be able to access the sensitive information.
1.1.2. Con: All your server logic is now running right in your web or mobile client, which makes it easier to hack.
1.2. Con: It is hard to access data given it is hosted on the Firebase server.
1.3. Con: Even if the devs can't access the database, Google can still do it.
1.3.1. Pro: This helps to cement Google's monopoly and control in the industry and over our every-day lives.
1.3.1.1. Pro: google just aims at accumulating more and more power
1.4. Pro: Firebase supports offline operations.
1.5. Con: Google can still theoretically get breached.
1.5.1. Con: It is far less likely that Google, with access to the funds and talent needed to build a foolproof security system, will get breached than a firm with fewer resources at its disposal.
1.6. Pro: Firebase is good to use for beginners.
1.6.1. Pro: Google Firebase have a basic plan that is completely free.
1.7. Pro: Google Firebase is a very good option if you're a lazy dev.
1.7.1. Pro: Firebase doesn't require as much effort compared to creating your own database.
1.8. Pro: Firebase is designed with scalability in mind.
